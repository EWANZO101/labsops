What is RIDERSAFE?

RIDERSAFE is a free app that helps keep you and your riding mates safe when you're out on the road.

Think about it ‚Äî when you're riding with a group, what happens if someone comes off their bike on a quiet back road? How do the others know who to call? Where exactly did it happen? Precious minutes can be lost trying to figure this out.

RIDERSAFE solves this. It's like a safety net for your riding group. Everyone's emergency contacts are stored and accessible, you can see where everyone is in real-time, and if the worst happens, one tap sends an alert with your exact location to everyone in your group.

No complicated setup. No monthly fees. Just peace of mind every time you throw a leg over.

How RIDERSAFE helps when it matters most

You're out on a Sunday ride with five mates. Beautiful day, quiet country roads, everyone's having a great time. You're at the back of the group when you see Dave up ahead go wide on a corner. Gravel. He's down.

Without RIDERSAFE: Everyone stops. Someone's trying to call 999 but can't describe exactly where you are. Another mate is going through Dave's jacket looking for his phone to call his wife. Precious minutes tick by. The ambulance struggles to find you.

Someone else opens Dave's profile and calls his wife directly. You can even share his medical info with the paramedics when they arrive.

The outcome: Help arrives faster. Dave's family is contacted immediately. Everyone knows exactly what to do because it's all right there in the app. That's what RIDERSAFE is for ‚Äî being prepared for the moments you hope never happen.
